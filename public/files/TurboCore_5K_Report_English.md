## 🧠 How the AI Brain Works

TurboCore runs **10 ML agents** that talk to each other every day at market close.

### Step 1 — Read the Market (Regime Detection)
The AI classifies the market into one of 3 states:

| State | What it means | What TurboCore does |
|-------|--------------|---------------------|
| 🟢 **BULL** | Trend is up, momentum strong | Go aggressive with TQQQ |
| 🟡 **SIDEWAYS** | Choppy, no clear trend | Stay defensive with QQQ/QLD |
| 🔴 **BEAR** | Market breaking down | 100% cash in SGOV (T-bills) |

### Step 2 — Score the Signal
An XGBoost AI scores every buy/sell signal from 0–100%.
- Score **> 65%** = Full aggressive allocation 🚀
- Score **50–65%** = Moderate position ⚖️
- Score **< 50%** = Skip the trade ⏸️

### Step 3 — Pick the Right Mix
Instead of being all-in on one thing, TurboCore **blends 4 assets** based on the regime:

| Regime | QQQ | QLD (2x) | TQQQ (3x) | SGOV (Cash) |
|--------|-----|----------|-----------|-------------|
| 🟢 BULL Aggressive | 40% | 0% | 60% | 0% |
| 🟡 SIDEWAYS Defensive | 66% | 15% | 0% | 19% |
| 🔴 BEAR | 0% | 0% | 0% | **100%** |

### Step 4 — Size It with Math
Uses the **Kelly Criterion** (at ¼ size for safety) to size each position — bigger bets when confident, smaller when uncertain.

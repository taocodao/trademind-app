## 🤖 How the AI Works (Without the Jargon)

TurboCore Pro runs **three independent AI layers** that must agree before any aggressive move is made. Think of them as three different expert analysts voting on every decision:

### Layer 1 — The Market Radar 🌐
Continuously scans not just price and momentum, but also cross-market signals including volatility futures, credit market conditions, and market breadth indicators. Classifies the market into one of three states:
- 🟢 **BULL** — Conditions favorable, deploy LEAPS leverage
- 🟡 **SIDEWAYS** — Mixed signals, hold stable ETF positions

Unlike simple moving averages, this radar can **predict** a regime shift 5–20 days *before* it happens — the key difference that kept TurboCore Pro positive in 2022 while everything else crashed.

Every time the market gives a "buy signal," this layer analyzes 30+ market characteristics to score the signal quality from 0 to 100%. It is specifically trained to recognize **"dead cat bounces"** — fake rallies during bear markets that trap investors into losses. Low-quality signals are simply skipped.

| 🟡 Moderate (60–75%) | Deploy partial LEAPS + ETF blend |

### Layer 3 — The Allocation Optimizer ⚙️
